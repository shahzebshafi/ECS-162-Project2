Coin Toss Simulator

Click the button to toss a coin!

Heads and Tails will be displayed on the screen

The amount of heads and tails will be tracked per session

Rock, Paper and Scissors

Click on the button of the rock, paper or scissors to play against the computer

Ways to win (bold wins): Paper beats Rock, Rock beats Scissors, Scissors beats Paper

Ways to lose is vive versa (bold loses): Paper loses to Scissors, Rock loses to Paper, Scissors loses to Rock

Tie happens if computer and you have the same choice

The amount of wins for the user and the computer will be tracked per session

Snake Game

Use the arrow keys to move the snake

Eat the food to grow the snake

Running into yourself or the boundaries will cause the game to end

The amount of food eaten and blocks traveled will be tracked per play
